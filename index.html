Welcome to Squeakies

Opened in January 2016, owned and run by an highly dedicated experienced team in the Education and Care sector.

Squeakies provides top quality education and care, within a warm, safe and nurturing environment. We are  highly trained in safeguarding our children, within an environment that celebrates diversity and promotes equality.  Our beautiful grounds allow us to learn the majority of the time outside, with our kitchen garden, den building area, sensory space, brook and a plethora of natural learning experiences.

Regular Activities

Development & Learning

Squeakies was born and developed from our love of nature. It is widely known that learning in nature offers numerous benefits, enhanced cognitive function, improved mental and physical health and the development of social skills. Natural spaces encourage child-led learning, where children are given the freedom to decide how they will learn… muddy puddles to jump in, sticks to build dens, tree canopies to shelter under. Added to that the wildlife, ladybirds, beetles, slug and snails, we have an abundant canvas to feed our children’s imagination.

Food & Kitchen Garden

At Squeakies we love growing our own fruit and vegetables in our Kitchen Garden. We use our own produce in the meals we provide for our children. Children are encouraged to take responsibility for the process from planting to picking! We are proud to hold Level 4 award in gardening with the Royal Horticulture Society. Children are provided with 3 meals and 2 snacks a day, with free access to milk and water. We cater for all dietary requirements.
